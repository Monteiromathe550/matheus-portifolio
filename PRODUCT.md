# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Potential clients, founders, creators, and small businesses looking for a web designer or UX/UI designer. They arrive quickly, scan the work, and need to feel confidence in the designer's visual taste, process, and ability to create premium digital presence.

## Product Purpose

This portfolio presents Matheus Monteiro's design practice, selected projects, specialties, process, and contact path. Success means the visitor understands the quality of the work, feels the brand as modern and creative, and moves naturally toward starting a project.

## Brand Personality

Modern, creative, precise, sophisticated, and calm. The interface should feel crafted and premium, with enough visual personality to avoid a generic template, but without distracting from the work.

## Anti-references

Avoid generic agency templates, loud effects for their own sake, abrupt motion, over-decorated cards, repeated gimmicky scroll reveals, and anything that feels like a stock landing page. The experience should not feel heavy, noisy, childish, or visually aggressive.

## Design Principles

1. Show taste through restraint and precision.
2. Make motion feel intentional, smooth, and tied to state or hierarchy.
3. Keep the portfolio easy to scan while preserving a memorable creative voice.
4. Let selected projects and typography carry credibility before decoration.
5. Preserve a premium dark visual system with subtle contrast and refined pacing.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Respect reduced-motion preferences with simplified movement rather than broken or abrupt states. Keep text legible against the dark background, preserve keyboard focus states, and make navigation and contact paths clear across desktop and mobile.
